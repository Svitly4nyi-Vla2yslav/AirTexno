Testing Sticky Mobile Bar

This page tests the sticky bar appearance logic. The bar should appear when you scroll past the hero section (when hero bottom is less than 20% of viewport height).

How it works

1. Find Hero element by ID: #header

2. Get its bounding rect: getBoundingClientRect()

3. Check if bottom edge is less than 20% of viewport: heroRect.bottom < window.innerHeight * 0.2

4. Show sticky bar when condition is true
